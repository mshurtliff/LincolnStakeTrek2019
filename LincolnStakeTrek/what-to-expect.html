What To Expect

The Trek is open for all to participate. For the young at heart there will be challenging trails, rivers, mountains, and valleys to pull a handcart through. For those less adventurous, there will be shorter hikes and other opportunities to learn about our pioneer heritage. There will be planned trail activities, a carnival, and spiritual firesides. We hope all will come with a desire to feel closer to their pioneer ancesters and understand some of the trials they faced and how the Lord blessed them.
