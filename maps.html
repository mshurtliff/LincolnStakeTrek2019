Trek Bucket Specifications

Everything you take on the trek will need to fit inside a 5 gallon bucket (not including your sleeping items) and weigh no more than 17 pounds. This document outlines the trek bucket requirements and the packing thereof. This document is also available as part of the Ma & Pa Handbook.
